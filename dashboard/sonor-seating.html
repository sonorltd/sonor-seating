Why dedicated seating

A cinema is only as good as the seat you watch it from.

The screen and sound get the attention, but the seating decides whether a room gets used every night or admired once a month. The right range fits your dimensions, seats everyone with proper sightlines, and reclines without touching the wall behind. We size it around your room first — then show only what genuinely fits.
